What We're Building Together

Closure-SDK (Walter's layer) verifies identity: compose ordered data on S³, detect divergence, classify as missing (W) or reorder (RGB). Static comparison of completed streams.
Closure Ea maintains identity: living lattice of Enkidu cells that compose events, learn through directional adapters, and spawn higher spheres from the data's own closure cadence.
Marvin Benchmark measures downstream reality: validation distance between stated intention and observed effect. Three pillars: coaching outcome, audience activation, system resilience.
The Marvin Benchmark uses Closure-SDK as its measurement instrument. Sigma is the score. Channel is the failure mode. The reference stream is the optimal path. The actual stream is what the model did.
Closure Ea upgrades this: instead of a pre-specified reference stream, a Gilgamesh lattice trained on real coaching conversations discovers what optimal disclosure paths look like from the data itself. The genome becomes the reference. No LLM-generated ground truth needed.
